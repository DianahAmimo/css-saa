An Algorithm Summarizes Lengthy Text Surprisingly Well An algorithm that summarizes long texts and huge documents impeccably has been developed by researchers at Salesforce. This algorithm uses machine learning technology to output coherent and accurate summaries of texts from bigger pieces. It also uses supervised learning technique and employs artificial attention to the text that it is absorbing and outputting. This ensures that the text it is supposed to summarize is well understood and the key points are captured. This algorithm can be of great use in a number of ways. The algorithm also uses reinforcement learning (self-learning: learning from its experiences) in order to complete the text summarization perfectly. This application can be of great gain when used in Cytonn Investments. With the art of reading/reading culture being cultivated in the company, the application will enable the team to be reading a lot of documents, texts and articles within a short time. It will provide the users with the key points about the book to be read and ensure that they have the key points to focus on as they begin reading the entire book. This will make the book reading session more lively and interesting. It will also be helpful when research is being conducted. The application will provide a summary of specific research sources to the user, enabling them to easily weigh and select the source that is rich in content in accordance to the research topic being covered. In line with that, it will also lead to cost saving when it comes to purchasing the research sources in that, the company will only pay for the sources that are rich in content and will be actively used in the research.
